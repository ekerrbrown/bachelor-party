Rules

I know rules are a buzzkill, but to make this weekend the most fun it can be, I must set some baselines.

Be Respectful

Whether your mindset is “This is woke P.C. created by the libs” or “I need a safe space”, we can all agree that this weekend is about me. What I want is to have as much fun as possible with the people I love, so please, none of the following: Do not use any phrases that can be discriminatory toward sexual orientation, race, and/or disabilities. You do not know every person’s story and what they consider hurtful/offensive. Shoot the shit but don’t cross any lines. No fighting. While I doubt this will be an issue, I still want to clarify that I do not want any skirmishes within the group or externally. If you have a problem, mediate it or vent to someone you know there (including me).

Control Your Liquor

While I do want us to have fun and let loose, we all need to be responsible enough with our drinking and actions. I am not afraid to put you on a drinking ban if a night goes too wonky because of you, and I hope you will hold me to the same standard.

Be Financially Responsible

Unfortunately, no one here came from families where we were spoon-fed hundred-dollar bills. Flights and hotels might be expensive. I appreciate you just making the trip. If you can’t make it, or if you can only come for a night or two, I will understand. I want to keep it relatively cheap once we get there. The only activities I have on the agenda are bowling and Dave and Busters and Axe Throwing. All are optional, but I would like it if you were at least there if you do not want to participate. Food/drinks will be split based on what you order and calculated at the end of the trip. I want you to make this trip but not empty the bank.
